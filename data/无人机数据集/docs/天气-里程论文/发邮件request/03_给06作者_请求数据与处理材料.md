# 给 06 作者的邮件

## 收件人
- `verdiana.bottino@unina.it`

## 主题
- `Request for dataset for drone battery discharge prediction study`

## 邮件正文
```text
Dear Dr. Bottino,

I am Yansong Zhang, a third-year undergraduate student at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

I am currently studying weather-related and operational effects on quadrotor energy consumption, and I hope to further develop this topic for research learning and student competitions. I have been reading your paper, “A data-driven learning method for online prediction of drone battery discharge” (Aerospace Science and Technology, 2022, DOI: 10.1016/j.ast.2022.107921), because it is highly relevant to battery discharge prediction for drone operations.

In the article, the Data Availability section states that the data will be made available on request. I would therefore like to kindly ask whether you could share the dataset used in the study, if possible.

I am especially interested in the following materials:

1. The telemetry data collected during the standard-path and generic-path flights.
2. The ground-collected weather data used together with the flight data.
3. Any processed training/testing data derived from the raw experimental data.
4. If available, any scripts or implementation details used to prepare the dataset for the learning method.

My purpose is academic study and method learning only. I will use the materials strictly for research learning, reproducibility study, and student competition exploration, and I will properly cite your paper.

Thank you very much for your time and consideration.

Best regards,
Yansong Zhang
Third-year Undergraduate Student
Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications
Alternative contact email: 1839369370@qq.com
```

## 发送说明
- `06` 这篇正文只明确写了“数据可申请”，没有像 `05` 那样直接写“代码可申请”。
- 所以正文里以数据为主，脚本只作为附带请求，这样更稳，不容易让对方觉得你要得太满。

# 逐句翻译：背景与摘要（第一版）

说明：这是手工逐句翻译样稿，当前先完成题目与 `Background & Summary` 部分，后续继续扩展。

## 题目
- 原文：`In-flight positional and energy use data set of a DJI Matrice 100 quadcopter for small package delivery`
- 译文：`面向小型包裹配送的 DJI Matrice 100 四旋翼无人机飞行位置与能耗数据集`

## Background & Summary

### 第 1 段
- 原文：`The last decade has seen considerable development towards using Uncrewed Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) or "drones" to improve the efficiency, speed, and access of last-mile package delivery.`
- 译文：过去十年，利用无人驾驶飞行器（UAV）或“无人机”来提升末端包裹配送效率、速度和可达性的研究与实践取得了显著发展。

- 原文：`Apart from the ability to carry packages, the majority of these UAVs also have the capability to takeoff and land vertically to reduce their operational footprint and increase their delivery precision.`
- 译文：除了能够运送包裹之外，这类无人机中的大多数还具备垂直起降能力，从而可以缩小其运行占地并提高投送精度。

- 原文：`A common solution is to use a multirotor system that generates lift by pushing the air down by using a set of brushless direct current (BLDC) motors that spin counter-rotating propellers.`
- 译文：一种常见方案是采用多旋翼系统，通过一组无刷直流电机（BLDC）驱动反向旋转的螺旋桨向下推动空气，从而产生升力。

- 原文：`UAVs that produce lift purely using rotors either during the entirety of the mission or for a part of it, need considerably large amounts of energy to lift a package when compared to a conventional winged aircraft where lift is produced by air flowing over a fixed wing.`
- 译文：与依靠气流掠过固定机翼来产生升力的传统固定翼飞行器相比，那些在整个任务过程中或任务的一部分过程中完全依赖旋翼产生升力的无人机，在携带包裹时需要消耗显著更多的能量。

- 原文：`This larger energy requirement has a considerable impact on the range and endurance of these multi-rotor UAVs.`
- 译文：这种更高的能量需求会显著影响多旋翼无人机的航程和续航能力。

- 原文：`The energy required is a function of the environmental factors like the prevailing wind conditions, temperature, humidity, etc; UAV design parameters such as the powerplant specifications, and the shape and build of the UAV; and mission-specific parameters such as the nominal ground speed, operation altitude, and delivery package specifications.`
- 译文：所需能量取决于多类因素，包括盛行风、温度、湿度等环境因素，动力系统规格、无人机构型与结构等设计参数，以及标称地速、运行高度和配送包裹规格等任务参数。

- 原文：`Developing energy models that can accurately predict the energy use of these UAVs using these factors as inputs is critical in improving UAV safety and efficiency.`
- 译文：开发能够以这些因素为输入并准确预测无人机能耗的能量模型，对于提升无人机的安全性与运行效率至关重要。

- 原文：`Current research uses theoretical models and physical parameters to estimate energy consumption, while other methods use data-driven approaches to regressed parameters and estimate models.`
- 译文：当前研究中，一部分工作使用理论模型和物理参数来估计能耗，另一部分工作则采用数据驱动的方法，对参数进行回归并建立估计模型。

### 第 2 段
- 原文：`However, a recent survey on the state-of-the-art of energy modelling for multirotor UAVs compared various models and found that the even with the common sets of parameters, the energy consumption rate (J/m) varies by a factor of 3 to 5 across the models.`
- 译文：然而，近期一项关于多旋翼无人机能耗建模现状的综述比较了多种模型后发现，即使使用相同的一组参数，不同模型给出的单位距离能耗率（J/m）仍可能相差 3 到 5 倍。

- 原文：`The survey advocates for comparing results to empirical data from comprehensive drone delivery field tests to improve the energy prediction accuracy.`
- 译文：该综述主张将模型结果与全面的无人机配送实地试验经验数据进行对比，以提高能耗预测精度。

- 原文：`Most studies only conduct a few flight and the raw flight data is not released in public domain to aid comparisons.`
- 译文：大多数研究只进行了少量飞行试验，而且通常没有公开原始飞行数据，因而难以支持横向比较。

- 原文：`Given the strict regulatory requirements and significant effort required to conduct UAV field tests, no standard comprehensive data set exists in the public domain to the best of authors' knowledge.`
- 译文：考虑到无人机外场试验受到严格监管要求，并且执行成本与工作量都很高，据作者所知，公开领域中尚不存在标准化且全面的此类数据集。

### 第 3 段
- 原文：`In this work, we performed experiments in order to empirically measure the energy use of a Multirotor UAV with autonomous capabilities while carrying a range of payloads through various campaigns.`
- 译文：在本研究中，我们通过多轮试验，实测了一架具备自主飞行能力的多旋翼无人机在携带不同载荷时的能耗情况。

- 原文：`We autonomously direct a DJI Matrice 100 (M100) drone to take off, carry a range of payload weights through a triangular flight pattern, and land.`
- 译文：我们让一架 DJI Matrice 100（M100）无人机自主起飞，携带不同重量的载荷按照三角形航线飞行，并最终降落。

- 原文：`We collected high-resolution data on the inertial speeds, altitude, wind and energy for each flight.`
- 译文：对于每一次飞行，我们都采集了高分辨率的惯性速度、高度、风场和能量相关数据。

- 原文：`Between flights, we varied specified parameters, such as commanded ground speed, payload weight, and cruise altitude.`
- 译文：在不同飞行任务之间，我们改变了若干指定参数，例如指令地速、载荷重量以及巡航高度。

- 原文：`We simultaneously collected data from the broad array of on-board sensors throughout 209 flights.`
- 译文：在总计 209 架次飞行过程中，我们同步采集了来自多种机载传感器的数据。

### 第 4 段
- 原文：`We developed an experimental protocol to ensure the reliability of the data collected.`
- 译文：我们制定了一套实验流程，以保证采集数据的可靠性。

- 原文：`The flights followed a pre-established route with varying altitude (25 m, 50 m, 75 m and 100 m), speed (4 m/s, 6 m/s, 8 m/s, 10 m/s and 12 m/s) and payload mass (no payload, 250 g and 500 g).`
- 译文：所有飞行均沿预先设定的航线执行，并改变飞行高度（25 m、50 m、75 m 和 100 m）、速度（4 m/s、6 m/s、8 m/s、10 m/s 和 12 m/s）以及载荷质量（空载、250 g 和 500 g）。

- 原文：`Each combination was repeated at least three times, totaling 195 flights.`
- 译文：每一种参数组合至少重复三次，总计形成 195 架次飞行。

- 原文：`In addition, 14 recordings were performed with the drone in hover and idle modes, for a total 209 flights.`
- 译文：此外，我们还记录了 14 组无人机处于悬停与空转模式下的数据，因此总计达到 209 架次记录。

- 原文：`Finally, the data provided by each sensor were synchronized at a frequency of approximately 5 Hz.`
- 译文：最后，各个传感器提供的数据被同步到约 5 Hz 的频率。

- 原文：`Information on the operational setup, such as payload mass, altitude and speed during cruise, date and time the flight started, and the predefined route, was manually logged and attached to the data set.`
- 译文：关于运行设置的信息，例如载荷质量、巡航阶段的高度与速度、飞行开始的日期与时间，以及预定义航线，也被人工记录并附加到数据集中。

### 第 5 段
- 原文：`The flights were performed in the township of Penn Hills, PA at a site that is approximately 16 kilometers away from Pittsburgh, PA, USA.`
- 译文：这些飞行试验在美国宾夕法尼亚州 Penn Hills 镇的一处场地进行，该场地距离宾夕法尼亚州匹兹堡约 16 公里。

- 原文：`Proper certifications from federal and local authorities were obtained prior to testing.`
- 译文：在测试开始前，研究团队已获得联邦和地方主管部门的相应许可。

- 原文：`Checklists were created to ensure that flights would occur as safely and efficiently as expected.`
- 译文：研究团队还制定了检查清单，以确保飞行能够按照预期安全且高效地开展。

- 原文：`Finally, the information collected on each flight was plotted and assessed in order to validate the consistency of the data acquired.`
- 译文：最后，研究团队对每次飞行采集的信息进行了绘图和评估，以验证所获数据的一致性。

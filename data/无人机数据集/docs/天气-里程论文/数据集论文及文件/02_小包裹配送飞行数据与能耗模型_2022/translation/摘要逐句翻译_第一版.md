# 摘要逐句翻译（第一版）

## Summary
- 原文：`Uncrewed aerial vehicles (UAVs) for last-mile deliveries will affect the energy productivity of delivery and require new methods to understand energy consumption and greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions.`
- 译文：用于末端配送的无人驾驶飞行器（UAV）将改变配送系统的能量生产率，因此需要新的方法来理解其能耗和温室气体（GHG）排放。

- 原文：`We combine empirical testing of 188 quadcopter flights across a range of speeds with a first-principles analysis to develop a usable energy model and a machine-learning algorithm to assess energy across takeoff, cruise, and landing.`
- 译文：我们结合了 188 架次四旋翼飞行在多种速度条件下的经验测试，以及基于第一性原理的分析，构建了一个可用的能耗模型和一个机器学习算法，用于评估起飞、巡航和降落阶段的能量消耗。

- 原文：`Our model shows that an electric quadcopter drone with a very small package (0.5 kg) would consume approximately 0.08 MJ/km and result in 70 g of CO2e per package in the United States.`
- 译文：我们的模型表明，在美国场景下，一架运送极小包裹（0.5 kg）的电动四旋翼无人机，其能耗约为 0.08 MJ/km，每个包裹对应约 70 g CO2e 排放。

- 原文：`We compare drone delivery with other vehicles and show that energy per package delivered by drones (0.33 MJ/package) can be up to 94% lower than conventional transportation modes, with only electric cargo bicycles providing lower GHGs/package.`
- 译文：我们将无人机配送与其他交通工具进行了比较，结果表明，无人机的单包裹能耗（0.33 MJ/包裹）相较于传统运输方式最多可降低 94%；在单位包裹温室气体排放方面，只有电动货运自行车比无人机更低。

- 原文：`Our open model and coefficients can assist stakeholders in understanding and improving the sustainability of small package delivery.`
- 译文：我们的开放模型及其系数可帮助相关利益方理解并改进小型包裹配送的可持续性。

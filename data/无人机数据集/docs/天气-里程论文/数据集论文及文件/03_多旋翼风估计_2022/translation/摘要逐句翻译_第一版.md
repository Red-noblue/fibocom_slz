# 摘要逐句翻译（第一版）

## Abstract
- 原文：`Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) have benefited from a tremendous increase in popularity over the past decade, which has inspired their application toward many novel and unique use cases.`
- 译文：过去十年里，无人机（UAV）的普及程度大幅提升，这也推动了它们在许多新颖且独特场景中的应用。

- 原文：`One of them is the use of UAVs in meteorological research, in particular for wind measurement.`
- 译文：其中一个重要方向，就是在气象研究中使用无人机，尤其是用于风场测量。

- 原文：`Research in this field using quadcopter UAVs has shown promising results.`
- 译文：利用四旋翼无人机开展这类研究，已经展现出很有前景的结果。

- 原文：`However, most of the results in the literature suffer from three main drawbacks.`
- 译文：然而，现有文献中的大多数结果都存在三个主要缺陷。

- 原文：`First, experiments are performed as numerical simulations or in wind tunnels.`
- 译文：第一，很多实验是在数值仿真环境或风洞中完成的。

- 原文：`Such results are limited in their validity in real-life conditions.`
- 译文：因此，这些结果在真实环境中的有效性是受限的。

- 原文：`Second, it is almost always assumed that the drone is stationary, which limits measurements spatially.`
- 译文：第二，研究通常假设无人机处于静止状态，这会限制测量在空间上的覆盖能力。

- 原文：`Third, no attempts at estimating vertical wind are made.`
- 译文：第三，现有工作通常没有尝试估计垂直风。

- 原文：`Overcoming these limitations offer an opportunity to gain significant value from using UAVs for meteorological measurements.`
- 译文：如果能够克服这些限制，就有机会显著提升无人机在气象测量中的应用价值。

- 原文：`We address these shortcomings by proposing a new dynamic model-based approach, that relies on the assumption that thrust can be measured or estimated, while drag can be related to air speed.`
- 译文：为了解决这些问题，我们提出了一种新的基于动力学模型的方法，该方法假设推力可以被测量或估计，而阻力则可以与空速建立联系。

- 原文：`Moreover, the proposed method is tested on empirical data gathered on a DJI Phantom 4 drone.`
- 译文：此外，我们还在一架 DJI Phantom 4 无人机采集的实测数据上验证了该方法。

- 原文：`During hovering, our method leads to precision and accuracy comparable to existing methods that use tilt to estimate the wind.`
- 译文：在悬停状态下，我们的方法在精密度和准确度方面与现有基于姿态倾角估计风速的方法相当。

- 原文：`At the same time, the method is able to estimate wind while the drone is moving.`
- 译文：与此同时，该方法还能在无人机运动过程中估计风场。

- 原文：`This paves the way for new uses of UAVs, such as the measurement of shear wind profiles, knowledge of which is relevant in Atmospheric Boundary Layer (ABL) meteorology.`
- 译文：这为无人机的新用途铺平了道路，例如测量切变风廓线，而这类信息对大气边界层（ABL）气象研究具有重要意义。

- 原文：`Additionally, since a commercial off-the-shelf drone is used, the methodology can be replicated by others without any need for custom hardware development or modifications.`
- 译文：另外，由于本文使用的是现成商用无人机，因此其他研究者无需开发或改装专用硬件，也可以复现这套方法。

# 摘要逐句翻译（第一版）

## Abstract
- 原文：`This paper describes an adaptive method to predict the battery discharge of a multirotor drone over a generic path.`
- 译文：本文介绍了一种自适应方法，用于预测多旋翼无人机在任意路径上的电池放电情况。

- 原文：`A proper assessment of battery state of discharge trend is critical to ensure a safe operation of battery-powered aerial vehicles in critical environments, such as the urban ones.`
- 译文：对电池放电状态变化趋势进行恰当评估，对于确保电池驱动飞行器在城市等复杂环境中的安全运行至关重要。

- 原文：`Several standard paths were executed by a commercial, battery-powered drone to acquire the data needed to train a Deep Learning based method; telemetry files and ground-collected data were processed to train the proposed method according to a trajectory segmentation strategy.`
- 译文：研究使用一架商用电池动力无人机飞行了若干标准路径，以采集训练深度学习方法所需的数据；随后又依据轨迹分段策略，对遥测文件和地面采集数据进行了处理，以训练所提出的方法。

- 原文：`Two learning configurations were trained to predict the time-of-flight and the integral of the battery current needed to fly the standard path segments.`
- 译文：研究训练了两种学习配置，分别用于预测飞行标准路径段所需的飞行时间以及电池电流积分值。

- 原文：`The current integral for each standard path segment is exploited to estimate the corresponding reduction of the battery state of charge.`
- 译文：每个标准路径段的电流积分值被用来估计电池荷电状态的相应下降量。

- 原文：`Based on path segmentation into predefined standard sections, the presented solution allows to predict time of flight and battery consumption along any generic path.`
- 译文：基于将路径分割为预定义标准段的思路，本文提出的方法能够预测任意路径上的飞行时间和电池消耗。

- 原文：`This can be exploited to plan a complete path at strategic stage, as well as to estimate the remaining available power resource at any intermediate point along a generic trajectory both at strategic stage and during mission execution to be exploited by the drone operator or by a traffic management service.`
- 译文：这一能力既可用于在战略规划阶段设计完整路径，也可用于在战略阶段或任务执行过程中，估计任意轨迹中间点的剩余可用电量，以供无人机操作者或交通管理服务使用。

- 原文：`To validate the technique, a scaled package delivery mission is presented as an example of contingency management application.`
- 译文：为了验证该技术，文中给出了一个缩比包裹配送任务，作为应急管理应用示例。

- 原文：`The maximum distance that the drone can reach from selected points along the mission path was computed according to the remaining battery level.`
- 译文：研究根据剩余电量，计算了无人机从任务路径上选定点出发所能到达的最大距离。

- 原文：`Considering a random distribution of safe-landing areas around the planned path, the computed maximum distance was used to determine which safe-landing areas can be reached by the drone from a generic point of the path after an unexpected event.`
- 译文：在假设规划路径周围安全降落区呈随机分布的情况下，研究利用上述最大可达距离，判断无人机在发生突发事件后从路径上任意位置出发能够到达哪些安全降落区。
